About Me

My name is Gonçalo Ferreira. I'm an emphatic User Experience Designer from Portugal with a background in pastry and customer service. I am currently living in Berlin, but I am open to relocating.

I was a Pastry Chef for the past seven years until transitioning to the UX Design industry. I studied with CareerFoundry in 2021, specializing in Frontend Web Development for Designers.

I'm curious and passionate about everything I do and look forward to making an impact in this world and applying my skills and experience to create intuitive digital products more human-centric.

I'm an extremely hard worker, creative, a team player, adapt easily to any environment or situation, and never afraid to express my feelings and ideas at any moment.

How working in a kitchen made me a better designer?

Working every day in a kitchen made me ready to manage and deal with the unexpected and to be adaptable to any situation. It improved my attention to detail, made me a better team player under intense conditions, and allowed me to think out of the box to create different and new solutions. I believe coming up with new recipes developed and fostered my creativity, which I now apply to my projects.
